Privacy Policy

Student Help Club mein hum aapki privacy ka poora khayal rakhte hain. Hum wahi data collect karte hain jo aapke order ko deliver karne ke liye zaroori hai.

1. Cookies aur AdSense

Humari website Google AdSense ka upyog karti hai. Google, ek third-party vendor ke roop mein, aapke interests ke mutabik ads dikhane ke liye cookies ka istemal karta hai. Aap apne browser settings se ise control kar sakte hain.

2. Personal Information

Hum aapka naam, WhatsApp number aur subject codes tabhi collect karte hain jab aap checkout karte hain. Hum aapka data kisi teesre paksh (Third party) ko nahi bechte.
