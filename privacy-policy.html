Privacy Policy

At Student Help Club, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our website and services.

1. Cookies and AdSense

Our website uses Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements to our users. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your Google Ad Settings or browser configuration.

2. Personal Information Collection

We collect personal information such as your name, WhatsApp number, and subject codes only when you initiate a purchase or request support. This data is strictly used to facilitate the delivery of your academic resources and provide customer assistance.

3. Data Storage & Comments

When you post a comment on our Important Update articles, the name and text you provide are stored in our secure database to foster a community discussion. These comments are public; therefore, we advise you not to share sensitive personal details in the comment section.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure your data remains safe. We never sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please feel free to reach out to our team.
