Refund & Return

Thank you for choosing Student Help Club (SHC) for your academic needs. Please read our refund and return policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Digital Product Policy

Since Student Help Club provides non-tangible, irrevocable digital goods (PDF Solved Assignments and Study Materials), we do not issue refunds once the order is completed and the product link or file has been delivered. As a customer, you are responsible for understanding this upon purchasing any item from our site.

2. Exceptions & Replacements

While we do not offer monetary refunds, we are committed to student satisfaction. A replacement file may be provided under the following circumstances:

  • The delivered PDF file is corrupted or unreadable.
  • The subject code delivered does not match the subject code ordered.
  • There is a verified major technical error in the content provided.

3. Order Processing

All digital orders are typically delivered instantly via WhatsApp or Email after payment verification. If you do not receive your file within 24 hours of payment, please contact our support team immediately.

4. Contact Support

If you experience any issues with your purchase or have technical questions about the assignment content, our team is here to help.
